About EDUCORE

Your trusted partner for stationery, PPE, hygiene, and office furniture.

EDUCORE Stationery and Hygiene Supplies (Pty) Ltd is a South African company dedicated to providing high-quality stationery, office furniture, PPE, and hygiene products to educational institutions, government departments, and corporate clients.

With a focus on reliability and bulk supply, we support tenders and large-scale procurement with competitive pricing and nationwide delivery.

Our Values: Integrity, Quality, and Service Excellence.
